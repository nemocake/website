STATUS / COMING_SOON

schematic scores.

a three.js generator that rolls visual music scores from five vocabularies — cage, penderecki, de cesare, lepold-inspired geometric structures, and mathematical equations ranging from basic geometry to differential equations to asce stress/failure calculations. visitors will be able to roll their own compositions, right here, on this page.
